Instructions

You are about to participate in an experiment that includes {{C.NUM_MARKETS}} independent sequential markets consisting of up to {{C.NUM_PERIODS}} periods each. You start each market with your earned endowment of {{C.ENDOWMENT}}. One market will be randomly chosen for payment. The chosen market will be revealed to everyone only at the end of the experiment. Your earnings in the chosen market will be paid to you in private (by bank transfer).

Each of the market rounds proceeds as follows:

One non-divisible token is sold and resold (up to) {{C.NUM_PERIODS}} periods.

The possible values are publicly announced on the screen from the beginning and throughout the market. Every participant in the market has identical prior information. The default value of the token is zero. If another value is possible, it is shown on the {{if information == 'AMBIGUITY'}}screen, but no probability of occurrence of this second value is revealed at any time {{else}}screen.{{endif}}

There are {{C.PLAYERS_PER_GROUP}} participants in the market throughout the experiment. You are one of them. The composition of the market participants never changes.

In each period, you and the other {{number_others}} participants bid to buy or decide not to bid for the token at the announced period price. The following table shows you the prices for the token in each period.
