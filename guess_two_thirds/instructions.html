Task 1: Pick a number

As a first task you should try to predict ⅔ of the average of numbers between 0 and 100 picked by the whole group. Everybody will individually pick a number between 0 and 100. The average of the {{ C.PLAYERS_PER_GROUP }} numbers picked by the {{ C.PLAYERS_PER_GROUP }} group members is calculated (the sum of the {{ C.PLAYERS_PER_GROUP }} numbers is divided by {{ C.PLAYERS_PER_GROUP }}). Everybody gets 12 Euros for his/her pick, and whoever is closest to ⅔ of the average gets a bonus payment of an additional 5 Euros at the end of the experiment.
